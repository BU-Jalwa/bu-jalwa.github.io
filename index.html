Boston's Premiere Co-Ed Bollywood Fusion Dance Team

Hailing from the city of champions, Boston University Jalwa encompasses various dance styles (including contemporary, Bollywood, Bhangra, classical, and hip hop) to produce a set that is meaningful to us and our audience.
Since its establishment in 2004, Jalwa has sought to fulfill its mission of spreading Desi culture and dance, garnering countless awards and accolades along the way. Jalwa focuses on combining the cultural facets of South Asia with urban, modern dance from America to represent the identity of our community at large.
